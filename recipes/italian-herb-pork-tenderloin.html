Italian Herb Pork Tenderloin

With buttered egg noodles and sautéed zucchini

This is the kind of dinner that feels a little elevated without becoming fussy. The pork is seasoned with Italian herbs, roasted until juicy, and served with simple buttered noodles and zucchini.

Ingredients

  • Pork tenderloin
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Egg noodles
  • Butter
  • Zucchini
  • Montreal Chicken seasoning

Instructions

  1. Season the pork tenderloin with olive oil, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sear in a hot skillet, then roast until the center reaches 145°F.
  3. Boil egg noodles and toss with butter.
  4. Sauté zucchini with olive oil and Montreal Chicken seasoning.
  5. Rest the pork, slice, and serve with noodles and zucchini.

Cook's Notes

Pork tenderloin is best pulled around 145°F and rested before slicing. The zucchini should be browned but not mushy.
